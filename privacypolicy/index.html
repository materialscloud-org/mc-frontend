Privacy Policy (Personal Data Protection)

Materials Cloud Privacy Policy (Personal Data Protection) v1.3, March 2021

Scope and Definitions

This Privacy Policy shall apply to the personal data of the Users collected by Materials Cloud in connection with Materials Cloud Services and to any personal data posted or processed by the Users on Materials Cloud Website. Words in this Privacy Policy shall have the same meaning as defined in Article 2. “Definitions” of the Materials Cloud Terms of Use.

Data Controllers

For the personal data of the Users, which are collected by Materials Cloud for the User's account and the operation of the Services by Materials Cloud: info@materialscloud.org on behalf of Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Station 1, 1015 Lausanne, Switzerland. For any other personal data in particular any personal data posted and/or processed by the Users on Materials Cloud Web site, the respective User shall be the data controller. If you post, collect or otherwise process any personal data on the Website, you shall be solely responsible for the processing of such data in compliance with all applicable legal requirements.

Types and Use of Data collected

Materials Cloud collects personal data such as your name, e-mail address and other information you give when creating an account on Materials Cloud website. For operation and maintenance purposes, Materials Cloud may collect files that record interaction with Materials Cloud website (System Logs) or use for this purpose other personal data such as your IP Address. Materials Cloud only uses your personal data for the operation of Materials Cloud website and services as well as to improve Materials Cloud website and services. Other personal data may be collected on Materials Cloud website along dedicated explanation text contextually with any such data collection. Materials Cloud may use your contact data to send you information about Materials Cloud services.

Cookies

Materials Cloud does not store cookies. Materials Cloud uses Plausible to track the usage of the website without collecting any personal data or personally identifiable information. The Plausible data policy is compliant with different cookie laws and privacy regulations such as GDPR, CCPA and PECR.

Mode and place of processing the personal data

Methods of processing

Materials Cloud processes the User's personal data in a proper manner and shall take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the data.

Place

Materials Cloud computing infrastructure is hosted at the Swiss National Supercomputing Center (CSCS) in Lugano, Switzerland; hence, your personal data processed by Materials Cloud is stored at said place. If you access to the Services from a place outside of Switzerland, you understand that your data is then transferred to you abroad. For further information, please contact the data Controller as indicated above.

Retention time

The personal data processed by Materials Cloud is kept as long as you or Materials Cloud has not cancelled your account though Materials Cloud may keep the data after cancellation of your account as long as necessary for technical reasons (like backups) or to comply with legal requirements.

The rights of users

Users have the right, at any time, to know whether their personal data has been stored and can consult the data Controller to learn about their contents and origin, to verify their accuracy or to ask for them to be supplemented, cancelled, updated or corrected, or to block any data held in violation of the law, as well as to oppose their treatment for any and all legitimate reasons. Requests should be sent to the data Controller as set out above.

More details concerning the collection or processing of your personal data by Materials Cloud may be requested from the data Controller at any time. Please see the contact information at the beginning of this document.

Materials Cloud reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time by giving notice to the users or organizers on this page. It is strongly recommended to check this page often. If a user or organizer disagrees to any of the changes to the Policy, she/he must cease using Materials Cloud website and services and can request that the data Controller removes her/his personal data. Such a removal will result in a termination of your account.
